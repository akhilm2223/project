Frequently Asked Questions

What makes your tours eco-friendly?

Our tours prioritize minimal environmental impact, support for local communities, and conservation efforts. We use eco-certified accommodations, employ local guides, and offset carbon emissions from travel.

How physically demanding are your tours?

We offer a range of tours for different activity levels, from leisurely walks to challenging hikes. Each tour description includes a detailed activity level rating to help you choose the right experience.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes! We can accommodate most dietary needs including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and allergies. Please let us know your requirements when booking.
