Our Eco-Friendly Destinations

We carefully select destinations that offer authentic experiences while supporting conservation efforts and local communities. Each location has been vetted for its commitment to sustainable practices.

Costa Rica

With its incredible biodiversity and commitment to conservation, Costa Rica is a premier eco-travel destination. Explore rainforests, volcanoes, and beaches while staying in eco-lodges that minimize environmental impact.

Best Time to Visit: December to April (dry season)

View Costa Rica Tours

New Zealand

From the towering peaks of the Southern Alps to the pristine beaches of the North Island, New Zealand offers breathtaking natural beauty and a strong commitment to environmental protection.

Best Time to Visit: December to February (summer)

View New Zealand Tours

Iceland

With its renewable energy leadership and stunning landscapes, Iceland provides a unique blend of natural wonders and sustainable innovation. Witness geothermal activity, glaciers, and the Northern Lights.

Best Time to Visit: June to August (summer) or September to March (Northern Lights)

View Iceland Tours

Thailand

From community-based tourism in northern villages to marine conservation projects in southern islands, Thailand offers rich cultural experiences alongside natural beauty.

Best Time to Visit: November to February (cool, dry season)

View Thailand Tours

Galápagos Islands

This living laboratory of evolution is a premier destination for wildlife enthusiasts. Visit with carefully vetted operators who support conservation efforts and follow strict environmental guidelines.

Best Time to Visit: December to May (warmer waters, ideal for swimming)

View Galápagos Tours

Bhutan

This carbon-negative country offers pristine forests, ancient traditions, and a unique approach to development measured in Gross National Happiness rather than GDP.

Best Time to Visit: October to December (clear skies, festivals)

View Bhutan Tours

Our Sustainability Criteria

Each destination in our portfolio is selected based on the following criteria:

  • Strong local conservation initiatives
  • Availability of eco-certified accommodations
  • Support for local communities and economies
  • Opportunities for cultural exchange and education
  • Preservation of natural habitats and wildlife
