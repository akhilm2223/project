About Eco Travel Adventures

Founded in 2015, Eco Travel Adventures was born from a passion for exploring the world and a deep commitment to protecting it. Our mission is to provide unforgettable travel experiences that benefit both travelers and the planet.

Our Story

Eco Travel Adventures began when our founders, experienced travelers and environmental advocates, recognized the need for tourism that actively contributes to conservation and community development rather than depleting resources.

Starting with just two destinations and a handful of travelers, we've grown to offer experiences across six continents while maintaining our core values of sustainability, authenticity, and positive impact.

Today, we're proud to be recognized as leaders in sustainable tourism, having received multiple awards for our innovative approaches to eco-friendly travel.

Our Team

Our diverse team includes environmental scientists, tourism professionals, and local experts from around the world. Each team member brings unique insights and a shared passion for responsible travel.

All of our guides undergo extensive training in sustainable practices, local ecology, and cultural sensitivity to ensure they can provide informative, enriching experiences for our travelers.

Our Values

  • Environmental Stewardship - Minimizing our footprint and supporting conservation
  • Cultural Respect - Honoring and preserving local traditions
  • Community Benefit - Ensuring tourism supports local economies
  • Educational Experience - Fostering understanding and appreciation
  • Authentic Connection - Creating meaningful interactions

Certifications

  • Global Sustainable Tourism Council Member
  • Certified B Corporation
  • Carbon Neutral Certified
  • Rainforest Alliance Verified

Our Impact

We believe that travel should give back more than it takes. Here's how we're making a difference:

Conservation

5% of all tour proceeds go directly to local conservation initiatives. To date, we've contributed over $500,000 to protect endangered habitats and species.

Community Development

We've helped create over 200 sustainable jobs in local communities and support education programs in regions where we operate.

Carbon Reduction

Through carbon offsets and operational improvements, we've reduced our carbon footprint by 75% since our founding.
